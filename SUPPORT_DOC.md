# Support & FAQ: Orbit Bubble Puzzle

Welcome to the support page for **Loxley Orbit Bubble Puzzle**!

If you are experiencing any issues playing the game, or simply have questions about how the mechanics work, please refer to the frequently asked questions below.

## 🎮 How to Play
- **Objective:** Match 4 or more bubbles of the same color to pop them! The more you pop in a chain reaction, the higher your score.
- **Controls (Keyboard):**
  - **Left / Right Arrow Keys:** Move the falling bubbles horizontally.
  - **Up Arrow Key:** Rotate the bubbles clockwise.
  - **Down Arrow Key:** Drop the bubbles faster.
  - **Spacebar:** Hard drop (instantly snap bubbles to the bottom).
- **Controls (Mobile/Touch):**
  - Use the on-screen buttons below the game board to Move, Rotate, and Hard Drop.
- **Scoring:** Popping bubbles earns you points. Your highest score is automatically saved locally on your browser.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting & FAQ

**Q: Do I need an internet connection to play?**
A: No! Once you install the extension, Orbit Bubble Puzzle is fully playable offline.

**Q: Will my high score be saved if I close my browser?**
A: Yes, your highest score is saved locally within your browser's storage and will be there the next time you open the game.

**Q: The game window isn't fitting correctly on my screen.**
A: The game is fully responsive and designed to scale to your window. If things look cramped, try resizing your browser window or going fullscreen. The layout will adapt automatically!

**Q: The sound or music isn't playing.**
A: Check the cute circular toggle buttons at the bottom of the screen (or left side depending on layout). Make sure neither the music note 🎵 nor the sound effect 🔊 icon are greyed out. Also, ensure your computer's system volume is turned up!

**Q: How do I access the game after installing the extension?**
A: Simply click the cute Mascot icon in your Chrome toolbar (top right corner of your browser). The game will launch in a fresh, beautiful new tab for you to enjoy.
